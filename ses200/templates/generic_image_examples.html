Making Images Accessible

Generic Images

Generic images are powerful. They allow us to convey large amounts of information quickly and concisely. However, care must be taken to ensure that this information is not lost on individuals with visual impairments. A alterations to basic HTML code can ensure that you do not leave out a portion of your audience.

Ensure your images contain "alt" tags and captions.

A bad example of generic image use

Without any additional information, persons with visual impairments will be aware that an image is present but gain no context.

A good example of generic image use

These dogs are team players

By including the alt tags and caption, users are able to obtain a much clearer "picture" of the data represented.
